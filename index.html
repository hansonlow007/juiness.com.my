ABOUT US

At Juiness, we believe that health starts with what you drink. Founded in 2024, Juiness is a juice brand dedicated to bringing you fresh, nutritious, and delicious cold-pressed juices. Our name, inspired by "juice" and "freshness," reflects our commitment to offering beverages that nourish your body and delight your taste buds.

We specialize in cold-pressed technology, a method that retains the natural flavors and maximum nutrients from fruits and vegetables. Unlike traditional juices, our process ensures higher vitamin content, including Vitamin A, C, and B6, giving your body the boost it deserves—without any preservatives or artificial additives.

Our journey began as a local juice bar, quickly growing into a trusted name for healthy living. Today, Juiness is expanding beyond juice bars, bringing our nutritious drinks to hypermarkets and wellness centers, making it easier for everyone to enjoy health on the go.

At Juiness, we're not just selling juice—we're promoting a lifestyle. A lifestyle that's fresh, vibrant, and full of life. Stay fresh, stay healthy, stay Juiness.
